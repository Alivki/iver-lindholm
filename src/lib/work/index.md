Index is one of the sub-groups (*undergrupper*) inside [TIHLDE](https://tihlde.org), the student association for data engineering, digital infrastructure and cybersecurity, digital business development, digital transformation, and information processing at NTNU in Trondheim. I joined Index in July 2024 and work as a fullstack developer.

## What Index does

Per §4.4 of TIHLDE's bylaws, Index's main responsibility is to build and maintain the digital services TIHLDE's members rely on. The group works agile and is required to listen to and prioritize the needs of its members.

Concretely, Index is responsible for:

- Making sure every new member can register an account on TIHLDE's websites.
- Continuously developing and maintaining [tihlde.org](https://tihlde.org), the association's main site, along with related subdomains.
- Building other projects that may be useful to TIHLDE's members.

## My role

As a fullstack developer in Index I work across the stack on tihlde.org and the surrounding tooling, picking up tickets, reviewing PRs, and shipping features that the rest of the association actually uses day to day. It's been my first taste of working in a real codebase with other developers, and most of what I now know about React, Git workflows, and code review I learned here.
